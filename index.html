The Collection

Selangor's best houses were never built to fight the climate. They were built to work with it — deep eaves, cross breeze, timber louvers that breathe. We only list homes that still remember this.

Every house in the collection is inspected for orientation, airflow, and shade before it's inspected for anything else.
